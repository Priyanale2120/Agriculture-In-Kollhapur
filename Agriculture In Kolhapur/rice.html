RICE

Kolhapur district has a high cultivation rate in the hilly areas, where paddy is a major crop. Scented rice has always been a prized possession in regional economies since the days of yore. Different varieties of indigenous scented rice has been cultivated in different parts of India since ancient times. Both Charaka and Sushruta have extolled the medicinal values of fragrant rice in their respective treatise.

Rice is the major kharif crop grown in the district. The rice crop grown on 74% (112000 ha.) 0f the total area under cereal crops during the kharif season. Average productivity of the crop is 3029 kg/ha. Ranks 3 rd in the state.
